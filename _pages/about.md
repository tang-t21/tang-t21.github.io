Hi! I am Tian Tang (唐添). I received my Bachelor's degree from Yao Class, Tsinghua University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. [Mingyu Gao](http://people.iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/~gaomy/). During my undergraduate study, I also worked closely with Prof. [Baris Kasikci](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~baris/) and was fortunate to be advised by him. I have a broad interest in the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Computer Systems, with a focus on building efficient system support for AI applications.

I applied for Fall 2025 Ph.D. programs and received some offers, but eventually got AGI-hyped and joined an AI startup instead of pursuing a Ph.D. Sorry, [Baris](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~baris/).

I love connecting with people. If you are interested in my work or just want to chat, please feel free to drop me an email at **tiantang673@gmail.com**.
